# Voice & tone

Default copy voice for prototypes on this stack. Organisations should replace
this with their own product voice during /curate-manifest.

## Tone

- Plain, direct, confident. No exclamation marks in UI chrome.
- Sentence case everywhere: headings, buttons, labels, menu items
  ("New task", not "New Task").
- Speak to the user as "you"; the product never says "we".

## Terminology

- Pick one noun per concept and stick to it across every surface (if the
  domain says "task", never drift to "ticket" or "item").
- Verbs on buttons name the outcome: "Save changes", "Create task",
  "Delete project" — never "OK", "Submit", "Yes".

## Microcopy conventions

- Empty states: what this space is + how to fill it. "No tasks yet. Create
  your first task to get started."
- Errors: what happened + what to do. "Couldn't load tasks. Check your
  connection and retry."
- Destructive confirmations name the object: "Delete 'Q3 launch plan'? This
  can't be undone."
- Dates: relative within 7 days ("2 days ago"), absolute beyond ("12 Jun 2026").
- Numbers: locale-formatted, tabular alignment in tables.
